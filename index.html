Hidden Gem in Alfama

Nestled in the charming cobblestone streets near Castelo, LUDO'S offers a cozy escape from the bustling city. Our intimate brunch spot combines friendly Portuguese hospitality with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, creating an unforgettable dining experience in one of Lisbon's most authentic neighborhoods.
